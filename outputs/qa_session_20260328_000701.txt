Q1: What is the risk classification for self employed customers?
A1: According to section 4 of the document, "RISK CLASSIFICATION", medium risk customers include "self employed individuals and small business owners". Therefore, the answer is:

Medium risk customers.

Q2: How long must records be kept?
A2: According to the document, "All customer due diligence records must be maintained for a minimum period of seven years from the date of account closure." (Section 6. RECORD KEEPING)

Therefore, records must be kept for at least 7 years.

Q3: What documents do corporate customers need?
A3: According to the document, corporate customers must provide:

* Certificate of incorporation
* Memorandum of association
* Board resolution

(Reference: Section 3, CUSTOMER IDENTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS)

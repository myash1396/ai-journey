SUMMARY:
To approve retail loan applications, applicants must meet eligibility criteria of being at least 21 years old, having a minimum monthly income of Rs 25,000, a credit score of 700 or above, and two years of employment history. The maximum loan limits are Rs 10 lakhs for personal loans, Rs 50 lakhs for home loans, and Rs 15 lakhs for vehicle loans. Applications under Rs 5 lakhs require branch manager approval, while those above Rs 5 lakhs need regional credit committee approval; processing time is typically three to five working days for standard applications.
